Overview

Romania has a power score of 2.28, which puts it at rank 27 in the global power ranking, and rank 10 in the emerging markets power ranking. In comparison to 2021, Romania has improved in the power rankings by 55 places, from rank 82, to rank 27.

At 2.28, the power score of Romania is better than the regional average of 2.18 in the Europe region.

Power prices and costs

The average electricity price in Romania has increased from 172.9 USD/MWh in 2021 to 349.93 USD/MWh in 2022. Since 2017, the average electricity price in Romania has fluctuated between 119.07 USD/MWh (2017) and 349.93 USD/MWh (2022).
