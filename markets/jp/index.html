Overview

Japan has a power score of 2.14, which puts it at rank 33 in the global power ranking, and rank 21 in the developed markets power ranking. In comparison to 2021, Japan has improved in the power rankings by 17 places, from rank 50, to rank 33.

At 2.14, the power score of Japan is better than the regional average of 1.88 in the Asia-Pacific region.

Power prices and costs

The average electricity price in Japan has increased from 171.38 USD/MWh in 2021 to 233.55 USD/MWh in 2022. Since 2020, the average electricity price in Japan has fluctuated between 171.38 USD/MWh (2021) and 233.55 USD/MWh (2022).
