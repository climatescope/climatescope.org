Overview

Panama has a power score of 1.76, which puts it at rank 87 in the global power ranking, and rank 60 in the emerging markets power ranking. In comparison to 2021, Panama has deteriorated in the power rankings by -40 places, from rank 47, to rank 87.

At 1.76, the power score of Panama is lower than the regional average of 1.81 in the Latin America region.

Power prices and costs

The average electricity price in Panama has increased from 169.17 USD/MWh in 2021 to 193.03 USD/MWh in 2022. Since 2017, the average electricity price in Panama has fluctuated between 169.13 USD/MWh (2020) and 193.1 USD/MWh (2019).
