Overview

Indonesia has a power score of 1.672, which puts it at rank 95 in the global ranking, and rank 68 in the emerging markets ranking.Since 2021 Indonesia has deteriorated in the rankings by -4 places, from rank 91, to rank 95.

At 1.672, the power score of Indonesia is worse than the regional average of 1.89 in the Asia-Pacific region.

Power prices and costs

The average electricity price in Indonesia has dropped from 77.99 USD/MWh in 2021 to 75.06 USD/MWh in 2022. Since 2017, the average electricity price in Indonesia has fluctuated between 75.06 USD/MWh (2022) and 84.38 USD/MWh (2017).
